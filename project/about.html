About

Hi there and welcome to unveiled-folklore, thanks for stopping by!

I created this website to share the rich folklore and urban legends of El Salvador with the world. Growing up, I was always captivated by supernatural tales and mysterious legends that have been passed down through generations. This passion inspired me to create this space where these stories can live on and be discovered by people everywhere. El Salvador is a country full of history, culture, and enchanting tales waiting to be explored. Through this site, I hope to ignite your curiosity and perhaps even encourage you to visit El Salvador one day.
